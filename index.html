About

Yida Wang is a research scientist in the Parallel Computing Lab at Intel Labs. He received his Ph.D. in computer science and neuroscience from Princeton University. His research interest is in parallel computing and big data analytics. Yida currently works on applying cutting-edge computing techniques to analyze large-scale data, specifically the brain imaging data generated via fMRI.
